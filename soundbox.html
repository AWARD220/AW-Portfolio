Adobe XD

Recently Adobe XD came out with the addition to add video clips to prototypes, and to learn how to use this feature I decided to mimic the growing trend of vertical playback seen in apps like YouTube, Tiktok and Instagram. To house the videos, the Soundbox was created, a music-video playback app focusing on shorter interactions, influenced by the popularity of short form content. To further this, the app was prototyped with the intention of being compact, hiding certain content for only those who wanted to view it.
