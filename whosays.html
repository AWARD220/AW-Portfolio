Who Says Salad App

April 21’
Figma, Adobe Illustrator

Tasked with creating a take-away salad app, WhoSays, is a University project that involved researching both takeaway and salad services to create an experience that would allow for ease of use for a diverse group of users. To achieve this, I created an “exploded view” creator that allowed users to create their salads step by step, inspect the ingredients if needed. I also included a homepage for those who wanted to choose something prebuilt or an app recommendation.
