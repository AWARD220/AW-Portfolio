As a website I use a lot, Rate Your Music or RYM is quite a frustrating experience to view on mobile with the home page being the most major problem. Originally the home page is a place to see featured reviewers and artists however by being an exact port from the desktop version, The page finds itself to be extremely long and overcrowded, even needing a built in navigation tab to help users get past the massive wall of featured reviews. By changing the featured review to be scrolled horizontally, adding a more comprehensive menu and by cleaning up the UI, we create a much more modern and phone friendly experience.

All assets and reviews belong to rateyourmusic.com and the reviews taken for sample content.
